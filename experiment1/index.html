Experiment 1 - Bad Tinder Bios Generator

Description

This first experiment is based on my previous experience with heartbreak and the dating app Tinder. In order to make light of my situation and make it somewhat funny, I decided to make a bad Tinder biography generator. In the dating app Tinder, it offers a quick, witty, and oftentimes funny introduction to potential suitors, usually more satirical in nature rather than serious.

Technical

This project is a generator that is given a few basic names I’ve seen pop up more than often in my algorithm, things these people are looking for, then lastly red and green flags. These are used in a Javascript object to be passed into a randomized text generator to randomly create a small blurb in the format of a regular Tinder bio. The “find your match” button triggers a new generation of text for the user.

Reflection

Since this is a single person project, I thought I did a pretty good job, I followed the slides exactly and added a funny twist on the prompt. I understood that what we were trying to do was making something procedurally generated given several different suggestions for the generator to make. I think I learned most of the basics on how to get started and how to update the portfolio website we’re required to keep updated!
